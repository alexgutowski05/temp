How To Record and Submit Your Recordings


  1. On the navigation bar, click "Sheet Music". It will bring you to my Flat.io music page.
  2. Choose the song you would like to record. These are the songs you need to record.
    • This is the admin account. No songs are required.
  3. On the left, select "Download or Print" and "Download as MIDI"
  4. Go to SoundTrap and create an account.
  5. Scroll down to the "My Projects" panel, and click "Enter Studio"
  6. Select "Music
  7. Click "Import" and select the .mid file you just downloaded.
  8. Listen to the background track a few times to get the beat in your head
  9. Click "Add Track" and then "Voice and Microphones"
  10. WEAR HEADPHONES!!! If you don't wear headphones, you will have to re-record it.
  11. Press the big, red "Start Recording" button, and sing!
  12. When you are done, leave the microphone on for about 5 seconds. This will give me a sample of background noise that I can edit out. Adobe Audition scans the recording for the sample I give it, and tries to zap it out.
  13. Delete the .mid file you imported by clicking the 3 dots in the corner of it, and clicking "Delete Track".
  14. Press the purple "Save" button in the top middle of the page.
